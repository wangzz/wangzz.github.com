FocusAlarm Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 5, 2026

Wangzz (“we,” “us,” or “our”) values your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use FocusAlarm (the “App”).

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Alarm configurations (time, label, repeat rules, unlock task type)
  • Focus session records (duration, completion status)
  • User preferences and settings

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (model, OS version, language) for feedback diagnostics
  • App usage data (alarm configs, focus records, subscription status) stored locally

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification (name, ID number, phone number)
  • Precise location data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Photo content (camera is used only for real-time viewfinder in photo unlock; photos are never saved or uploaded)
  • Advertising identifiers

2. Permission Usage

Permission Purpose Required
Notifications Alarm ring alerts Yes; alarms cannot ring without it
Camera Photo unlock feature Optional; falls back to math unlock if denied

3. Third-Party Services

  • Apple StoreKit: In-app purchases and subscription management. Payment info is processed by Apple.
  • Apple iCloud: Data sync (if enabled), protected by Apple’s privacy policy.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device. iCloud-synced data is stored on Apple iCloud.
  • Data persists while the app is installed; uninstalling removes all local data.
  • No data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers.

5. Your Rights

  • Access and view your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your data (uninstalling removes all local data)
  • Revoke permissions in iOS Settings → FocusAlarm
  • Export a copy of your data

6. Children’s Privacy

This App is not intended for children under 14. Minors aged 14-17 should use the App under parental guidance.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be notified through in-app messages.
