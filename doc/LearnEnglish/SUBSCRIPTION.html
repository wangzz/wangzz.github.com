These Subscription Terms apply to subscriptions you purchase in LearnEnglish (the “App”) and supplement the Terms of Use. If these terms conflict with the Terms of Use, these Subscription Terms prevail.

1. Subscription Products

The App offers the following auto-renewable subscriptions (subject to current App Store availability):

Product Period Description
Monthly 1 month Unlocks all letters / phonics / words / sentences, full progress tracking, ad-free
Yearly 12 months Same as monthly, with a per-month discount
Lifetime One-time One purchase, permanent unlock of all current and future content (not a subscription; these terms govern its purchase flow and Parental Gate only)

Actual prices are those displayed in the App Store at the time of purchase and may vary by region, exchange rate, and Apple pricing.

2. Billing and Renewal

2.1 Auto-renewal: monthly and yearly subscriptions auto-renew. The subscription will renew for an additional period unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

2.2 Renewal charge: renewal fees are charged to your Apple ID within 24 hours before the end of the current period.

2.3 Renewal price: renewal price typically matches the price at initial subscription. If the price changes, Apple will notify you by email or in-app notice before the change takes effect.

2.4 Free trial: if a free trial is offered, the trial converts to a paid subscription at the end of the trial period unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the trial ends. A free trial is offered only to first-time Guardian subscribers, one per Guardian.

3. Managing Your Subscription

Manage or cancel your subscription at any time:

iOS Settings → your Apple ID avatar → Subscriptions → LearnEnglish → Cancel Subscription

Or open: https://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions

You can also go to in-app Settings → Manage Subscription to reach the same page.

4. Refund Policy

4.1 Per Apple App Store policy, all subscription refund requests must be submitted to Apple; we cannot process refunds directly.

4.2 Request refunds at https://reportaproblem.apple.com. Approval is at Apple’s discretion.

4.3 Cancelling a subscription does not refund the current period’s fee, but you retain access through the end of the current period.

5. Parental Gate

5.1 To comply with Apple App Store Guidelines 1.3 and 5.1.1 for children’s apps, the App requires a Parental Gate pass before the following operations:

  • Entering the Parent Center
  • Adjusting parent controls (daily time limits, audio preferences, push toggles, etc.)
  • Initiating a subscription purchase
  • Resetting learning progress
  • Opening external links (Privacy Policy, Terms of Use, these Subscription Terms, etc.)

5.2 The Parental Gate uses an adult-oriented math problem (e.g., “7 + 5 = ?”) rather than gestures or colors a child could guess, ensuring an adult performs the action.

5.3 After 3 consecutive failures, the gated function locks for 5 minutes to prevent brute-force attempts by children.
