Privacy Policy

BirthNotice - Birthday Reminder with Lunar Calendar

Last Updated: April 6, 2026

Introduction

BirthNotice (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

In short: Your data stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

Information We Access

Contacts (Optional)

  • What: When you choose to import birthdays from your phone’s contacts, the App accesses your device’s address book.
  • Why: To import names, birthday dates, phone numbers, and profile photos for birthday reminders.
  • Storage: All imported data is stored locally on your device only. We do not upload, transmit, or share your contact information with any server or third party.
  • Control: You can deny contacts access at any time in your device’s Settings. The App functions fully with manually entered data.

Notifications

  • What: The App requests permission to send local notifications.
  • Why: To remind you of upcoming birthdays at the times you configure.
  • How: All notifications are scheduled locally on your device using iOS’s built-in notification system. No push notification servers are involved.

Calendar Data

  • What: Birthday dates (both Gregorian and Lunar calendar) that you enter or import.
  • Storage: Stored locally on your device using Apple’s SwiftData framework.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be completely transparent. BirthNotice does NOT:

  • ❌ Collect any personal information
  • ❌ Track your location
  • ❌ Use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • ❌ Send any data to external servers
  • ❌ Use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • ❌ Share data with third parties
  • ❌ Display targeted advertising
  • ❌ Require account registration or login
  • ❌ Access your camera, microphone, or photos (unless you explicitly attach a photo to a contact)

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Birthday contacts and records: Stored using Apple SwiftData, in the App’s private sandbox.
  • Reminder settings: Stored in iOS UserDefaults, on your device only.
  • Notification schedules: Managed by iOS’s UNUserNotificationCenter, on your device only.

Data Backup

Your data may be included in your device’s iCloud or iTunes backup, as managed by Apple’s built-in backup system. We do not control or access these backups.

Data Deletion

  • Deleting a contact within the App permanently removes all associated data.
  • Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.

Children’s Privacy

BirthNotice does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The App does not require any personal information to function.

Third-Party Services

BirthNotice does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data processing services.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal data for us to provide, modify, or delete. All your data is under your full control on your device.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under GDPR. Since we do not collect or process personal data on our servers, these rights are inherently fulfilled.
