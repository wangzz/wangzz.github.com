Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

Q: How do I add a birthday?
A: Tap the “+” button on the Home or Contacts tab. You can enter the name, date (Gregorian or Lunar), group, and set custom reminders.

Q: How do I import birthdays from my contacts?
A: Go to Contacts tab → tap “+” → select “Import from Contacts”. The App will show all contacts that have birthday information. Select the ones you want to import.

Q: Why can’t I import contacts?
A: Make sure you have granted the App access to your contacts. Go to your device’s Settings → BirthNotice → Contacts → Allow. Also, only contacts with birthday information will appear in the import list.

Reminders & Notifications

Q: Will I receive reminders if the App is closed?
A: Yes! BirthNotice uses iOS local notifications, which are managed by the operating system. You will receive reminders even if the App is not running or your phone has been restarted.

Q: I’m not receiving notifications. What should I do?

  1. Go to your device’s Settings → Notifications → BirthNotice
  2. Make sure “Allow Notifications” is turned on
  3. Check that Alert Style is set to “Banners” or “Alerts”
  4. In the App, go to Settings → check that “Enable Reminders” is on
  5. Try “Reschedule All Notifications” in Settings

Q: How many reminders can I set?
A: iOS allows a maximum of 64 scheduled local notifications per App. You can set multiple reminders per person (e.g., 7 days before, 3 days before, and on the day). The Settings page shows your current notification count (e.g., “12 / 64”).

Q: Can I customize the reminder time?
A: Yes! When adding or editing a contact, you can freely set any number of days in advance and choose the exact time (hour and minute) for each reminder.

Lunar Calendar

Q: How does the Lunar Calendar feature work?
A: When adding a birthday, toggle “Use Lunar Calendar” and select the Lunar month and day. The App automatically converts the Lunar date to the Gregorian calendar for each year and schedules reminders accordingly.

Q: Why might the Lunar birthday date change each year?
A: The Lunar calendar does not directly correspond to the Gregorian calendar. A Lunar date (e.g., the 15th of the 8th month) falls on a different Gregorian date each year. The App automatically handles this conversion.

Greeting Messages

Q: How do I send birthday wishes?
A: Open a contact’s detail page or tap “Send Wishes” on a birthday card. Choose a style (Warm, Formal, Humorous, or Literary), select or edit a template, then tap “SMS”, “WeChat”, or “Copy” to send.

Q: Can I get more greeting templates?
A: Yes! Tap “Refresh” (🔄) in the templates section to load a new batch. The App includes 175+ templates in Chinese and additional templates in English, Japanese, Korean, and Spanish.

Data & Privacy

Q: Where is my data stored?
A: All data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload any data to external servers. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Q: How do I back up my data?
A: Your data is included in your regular iPhone/iPad backup (iCloud Backup or iTunes/Finder backup). When you restore your device from a backup, your BirthNotice data will be restored as well.

Q: How do I delete my data?
A: You can delete individual contacts by swiping left on the Contacts list, or by tapping “Delete” at the bottom of a contact’s detail page. Uninstalling the App removes all data.

Device Compatibility

Q: Which devices are supported?
A: BirthNotice requires iOS 17.0 or later. It works on:

  • iPhone (portrait mode)
  • iPad (portrait and landscape modes)

Q: Is there an Apple Watch version?
A: Not currently, but it is planned for a future update.

Troubleshooting

App crashes on launch

  1. Make sure your device is running iOS 17.0 or later
  2. Try force-quitting and reopening the App
  3. Restart your device
  4. If the issue persists, try reinstalling the App

Notifications stopped working

  1. Check Settings → Notifications → BirthNotice → Allow Notifications
  2. Make sure the App hasn’t been restricted by Focus mode
  3. Open the App → Settings → Reschedule All Notifications
  4. Check your notification count hasn’t reached the 64 limit

Contacts import shows empty list

  1. Verify contacts access is granted in device Settings
  2. Make sure your contacts actually have birthday fields filled in
  3. Only contacts with birthday information will appear
