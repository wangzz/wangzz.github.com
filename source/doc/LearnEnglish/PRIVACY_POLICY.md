# LearnEnglish 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

生效日期 / Effective Date: 2026-06-02


## English Version

wangzz ("we", "us", "our") places the highest priority on protecting the privacy of our users, especially minors and their parents/guardians. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share personal information when you use LearnEnglish (the "App"), and the choices you have. **Because the App is designed for children aged 3-5, we are committed to complying with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and equivalent international children's privacy regulations.**

### 1. Information We Collect

#### 1.1 Information You Provide

- **Parental control preferences** (set by the guardian): toggles for parent-only features, daily learning time limits, sound/language preferences
- **Learning progress**: the App locally records mastery of each letter/word/sentence (attempts, last-practice date) to unlock subsequent levels

#### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically (with explicit consent)

- **Voice recordings**: only after the user enters a "Repeat After Me" or "Pronunciation Practice" screen AND taps the microphone button will the App request microphone access and begin recording. Recordings are processed **on-device in real time** for pronunciation scoring; **recordings are never uploaded to any server**, and are destroyed from memory immediately after scoring — no file is written to local storage.

#### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does **not** collect:
- Real names, email addresses, phone numbers, physical addresses
- Account registration (no signup required)
- Precise location, contacts, photo library, health data
- Persistent device identifiers (except where Apple requires subscription receipt validation)
- Any form of advertising ID

#### 1.4 Content We Do Not Provide to Children

- No behavioral advertising
- No user-generated content (chat, comments, uploads)
- No social-media sharing SDK
- No requests for personally identifiable information

### 2. How We Use Information

- To provide core learning functionality (letter learning, pronunciation scoring, progress tracking)
- To store learning progress locally so the child can continue across sessions
- To respond to feedback and inquiries

We do **not** use personal information for any other purpose, and we do **not** sell personal information to third parties. We do **not** monetize children's information.

### 3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates only one third-party service, for Apple App Store subscription payment processing:

| Service | Provider | Data Collected | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| StoreKit 2 | Apple Inc. | Anonymized subscription receipts (managed by Apple) | Subscription billing and renewal |

**We do not integrate any third-party advertising SDK, analytics SDK, or social SDK.**

### 4. Storage and Security

- **Location of storage**: **All learning data is stored locally on your device** (via iOS UserDefaults and FileManager). We operate no backend server and store no children's personal information in the cloud.
- **Retention**: Local learning progress is retained only until you uninstall the App; uninstall deletes it.
- **Safeguards**: Voice recordings exist only in memory and are destroyed immediately after processing. The App makes no network calls except those required by Apple for subscription receipt validation.

### 5. Your Rights (exercised by the guardian)

Because the App does not collect personally identifiable information and does not require account registration, you (the guardian) have these rights:

- **View**: see your child's learning data in-app under Settings → Learning Progress
- **Delete**: tap Settings → Reset Learning Progress to clear all local data
- **Withdraw permissions**: at any time go to iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → LearnEnglish to revoke microphone access or delete local data
- **Uninstall**: uninstalling the App permanently removes all local data

### 6. Children's Privacy Protections

Because the App is designed for children aged 3-5, we apply these additional protections:

- **Verifiable parental consent (Parental Gate)**: before any operation that affects children (parent controls, subscription purchase), the App requires the user to complete an adult-oriented verification (math problem) to ensure an adult is performing the action
- **Data minimization**: we collect only the minimum information necessary for core functionality, and no personally identifiable information
- **No behavioral tracking**: no user profiling, targeted advertising, or remarketing
- **Plain language**: content visible to children uses simple, friendly, non-persuasive language
- **Dedicated children's privacy notice**: see [Children's Privacy Policy](https://github.com/wangzz/md-doc/blob/main/LearnEnglish/CHILDREN_PRIVACY.md)

### 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updated versions will be published in the GitHub `md-doc` repository with a revised Effective Date. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notice or release notes.
