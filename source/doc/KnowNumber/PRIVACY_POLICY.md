# 隐私政策 — 认识数字

**最后更新：2026-05-31**

## 概述

认识数字（KnowNumber）是一款专为 2-8 岁儿童设计的数字学习应用。我们高度重视儿童隐私保护，严格遵守《儿童在线隐私保护法》（COPPA）及相关法律法规。

## 数据收集

### 我们不收集的数据

- 本应用不收集任何个人身份信息
- 不收集位置信息、联系方式、通讯录
- 不使用任何第三方分析或广告 SDK
- 不向任何第三方传输用户数据

### 本地存储的数据

以下数据仅存储在您的设备本地，不会上传至任何服务器：

- 学习进度和关卡完成状态
- 每日使用时长统计
- 家长设置（年龄分组、时长限制、音效偏好）

## 家长控制

- 所有设置修改均需通过家长门禁（数学计算验证）
- 儿童无法自行修改年龄设置或关闭时长限制
- 家长可随时在"家长中心"查看学习进度

## 数据安全

- 所有数据存储在设备本地，受 iOS 系统级加密保护
- 本应用不使用网络请求，不存在数据泄露风险
- 卸载应用后，所有本地数据将被系统自动清除

## 儿童隐私承诺

- 本应用专为儿童设计，不收集 13 岁以下儿童的任何个人信息
- 家长门禁功能确保儿童无法未经授权访问设置和内购
- 应用内无任何社交功能或外部链接（家长中心除外）

## 变更通知

如本政策有更新，我们会在应用内通知您。

## 联系方式

如有任何隐私相关问题，请联系：knownumber@wangzz.com

# Privacy Policy — KnowNumber

**Last updated: 2026-05-31**

## Overview

KnowNumber is a number learning app designed for children aged 2-8. We take children's privacy very seriously and strictly comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and related laws and regulations.

## Data Collection

### Data We Do NOT Collect

- This app does not collect any personally identifiable information
- No location data, contact information, or address book access
- No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs
- No user data is transmitted to any third party

### Locally Stored Data

The following data is stored only on your device and is never uploaded to any server:

- Learning progress and level completion status
- Daily usage time statistics
- Parent settings (age group, time limit, sound preferences)

## Parental Controls

- All settings changes require parent gate verification (math calculation)
- Children cannot modify age settings or disable time limits on their own
- Parents can view learning progress at any time in the "Parent Center"

## Data Security

- All data is stored locally on the device, protected by iOS system-level encryption
- This app makes no network requests, eliminating any risk of data leakage
- All local data is automatically cleared by the system when the app is uninstalled

## Children's Privacy Commitment

- This app is designed for children and does not collect any personal information from children under 13
- Parent gate functionality ensures children cannot access settings and in-app purchases without authorization
- The app contains no social features or external links (except the Parent Center)

## Changes

We will notify you within the app if this policy is updated.

## Contact

If you have any privacy-related questions, please contact: knownumber@wangzz.com
