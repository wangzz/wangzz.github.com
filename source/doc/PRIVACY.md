# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 4, 2026**

## Overview

zz markdown ("the App") is a Markdown editor for macOS. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.** The App operates entirely offline on your local device.

- No user accounts or registration required
- No analytics or tracking
- No data transmitted to any server
- All files are stored locally on your device

## File Access

The App only accesses files that you explicitly open or create through the system file dialog. No files are accessed without your direct action.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
