# 隐私政策

**生效日期：2026-05-30**

KnowLetter（以下简称"本 App"）由 wangzz 开发并运营。我们深知儿童隐私的重要性，特此制定本隐私政策，说明我们如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。

## 一、信息收集

本 App **不收集任何用户数据**。

- 不收集姓名、年龄、性别等个人身份信息
- 不收集位置信息
- 不收集设备标识符（IDFA/IDFV）
- 不收集使用行为数据
- 不接入任何第三方分析 SDK
- 不接入任何广告 SDK

所有学习进度数据仅存储在设备本地（UserDefaults），不会上传至任何服务器。

## 二、信息使用

由于本 App 不收集任何用户数据，因此不存在信息使用行为。

## 三、信息共享

本 App 不与任何第三方共享用户信息。

## 四、儿童隐私

本 App 面向 2-6 岁儿童设计，符合 Apple Kids Category 要求：

- 不收集儿童个人信息
- 不向儿童展示第三方广告
- 不接入任何第三方 SDK
- 家长控制中心完全在设备本地运行

## 五、数据安全

所有数据存储在用户设备本地，不经过网络传输，不存在数据泄露风险。

## 六、隐私政策更新

我们可能不时更新本隐私政策。更新后的政策将在 App 内或 App Store 页面公布。

# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: 2026-05-30**

KnowLetter (hereinafter referred to as "this App") is developed and operated by wangzz. We understand the importance of children's privacy and have created this Privacy Policy to explain how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## 1. Information Collection

This App **does not collect any user data**.

- No personal identification information such as name, age, or gender is collected
- No location information is collected
- No device identifiers (IDFA/IDFV) are collected
- No usage behavior data is collected
- No third-party analytics SDKs are integrated
- No advertising SDKs are integrated

All learning progress data is stored locally on the device (UserDefaults) and is never uploaded to any server.

## 2. Information Use

Since this App does not collect any user data, there is no information usage.

## 3. Information Sharing

This App does not share user information with any third party.

## 4. Children's Privacy

This App is designed for children aged 2-6 and complies with Apple Kids Category requirements:

- No collection of children's personal information
- No third-party advertisements shown to children
- No third-party SDKs integrated
- Parental control center runs entirely on-device

## 5. Data Security

All data is stored locally on the user's device without any network transmission, eliminating the risk of data breaches.

## 6. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated policies will be published within the App or on the App Store page.
