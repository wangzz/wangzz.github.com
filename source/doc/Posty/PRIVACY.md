# 图记 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

## 中文版

wangzz（以下简称"我们"）高度重视用户隐私保护。本政策说明我们在您使用 图记（以下简称"本应用"）时如何收集、使用、存储、共享您的个人信息，以及您对这些信息的控制方式。请您在使用本应用前仔细阅读本政策。

### 1. 我们收集的信息

#### 1.1 您主动提供的信息

- 反馈与咨询信息：当您通过应用内「意见反馈」功能联系我们时，您主动填写的邮箱地址及反馈内容
- 您在使用本应用时产生的数据：设置偏好、操作记录等

#### 1.2 自动收集的信息

- **照片库访问**：当您授予相册权限后，本应用会读取您选中的照片用于编辑处理，不会上传至远程服务器
- **相机访问**：当您授予相机权限后，本应用会使用相机拍摄照片用于编辑处理
- **照片元数据**：本应用可能读取照片的 EXIF 信息（如拍摄时间、设备型号）用于展示，不会上传至远程服务器
- **设备信息**：设备型号、操作系统版本，用于确保功能兼容性
- **崩溃日志**：当应用发生崩溃时，本地记录崩溃信息用于问题定位，不会自动上传

#### 1.3 我们不会收集的信息

本应用不会收集以下信息，除非您主动授予对应权限：
- 精确地理位置
- 通讯录
- 健康数据

### 2. 我们如何使用这些信息

- 向您提供本应用的核心功能（图片编辑、滤镜处理、排版、导出等）
- 维护和改进产品服务质量
- 响应您的咨询与反馈
- 遵守适用的法律法规

我们不会将您的个人信息用于上述目的之外的用途，也不会向第三方出售您的个人信息。

### 3. 第三方服务

本应用集成了以下第三方服务，这些服务可能会收集必要的信息用于其声明的用途：

- **Apple StoreKit**：用于处理应用内购买和订阅。Apple 可能收集您的购买凭证和交易信息。隐私政策：https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
- **Apple iCloud**：当您启用 iCloud 同步时，数据通过 Apple iCloud 进行传输与存储。隐私政策：https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
- **Apple Vision Framework**：用于设备端 AI 图像识别（文字识别、主体提取），所有处理均在本地完成，数据不会离开您的设备
- **友盟SDK（Umeng SDK）**：用于应用数据统计与分析。友盟SDK 会收集设备信息（设备型号、操作系统版本、唯一设备标识符等）、网络信息、应用列表信息，用于提供统计分析服务。隐私政策：https://www.umeng.com/policy

我们已与上述第三方签署数据处理协议，要求其按照不低于本政策的标准处理您的信息。

### 4. 信息存储与安全

- 存储地点：您的数据主要存储在您的设备本地。涉及云端同步的部分存储于 Apple iCloud（若您启用）
- 存储期限：我们仅在实现本政策所述目的所必需的期间内保留您的个人信息
- 安全措施：我们采用加密传输、访问控制等技术措施保护您的信息

### 5. 您的权利

您有权：

- 访问和查看您的个人信息
- 更正不准确的个人信息
- 删除您的个人信息（通过卸载本应用或清除应用数据）
- 撤回您先前授予的权限（在系统「设置 → 隐私与安全性 → 图记」中调整）
- 导出您的数据副本

### 6. 未成年人保护

本应用的目标用户不包含未满 14 周岁的未成年人。如果您是未满 14 周岁的未成年人，请在监护人同意并陪同下使用本应用。我们不会在未获得监护人同意的情况下主动收集未成年人的个人信息。

### 7. 政策更新

我们可能会不定期更新本政策。更新后的政策将在本页面发布，并修改生效日期。重大变更我们会通过应用内通知或其他方式提示您。

### 8. 联系我们

如您对本政策有任何疑问、意见或投诉，请通过以下方式联系我们：

- 开发者：wangzz
- 邮箱：wzzvictory_tjsd@163.com

## English Version

wangzz ("we", "us", "our") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share your personal information when you use 图记 / Posty (the "App"), and the choices you have regarding that information. Please read this policy carefully before using the App.

### 1. Information We Collect

#### 1.1 Information You Provide

- Feedback and inquiries: When you contact us via the in-app "Feedback" feature, the email address and feedback content you voluntarily provide
- Usage data: Preferences and activity records generated while using the App

#### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Photo Library Access**: When you grant photo library permission, the App reads selected photos for editing. Photos are never uploaded to remote servers
- **Camera Access**: When you grant camera permission, the App uses the camera to capture photos for editing
- **Photo Metadata**: The App may read EXIF data (e.g., capture date, device model) for display purposes. This data is never uploaded to remote servers
- **Device Information**: Device model and OS version, used to ensure feature compatibility
- **Crash Logs**: When the App crashes, crash information is recorded locally for debugging. It is not automatically uploaded

#### 1.3 Information We Do Not Collect

Unless you explicitly grant the corresponding permission, we do not collect:
- Precise location
- Contacts
- Health data

### 2. How We Use Information

- To provide the core functionality of the App (photo editing, filter processing, layout, export, etc.)
- To maintain and improve product quality
- To respond to your inquiries and feedback
- To comply with applicable laws and regulations

We do not use your personal information for purposes beyond those stated above, nor do we sell your personal information to third parties.

### 3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services. These services may collect information necessary to fulfill their stated purposes:

- **Apple StoreKit**: Used for in-app purchases and subscriptions. Apple may collect your purchase receipts and transaction information. Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
- **Apple iCloud**: When you enable iCloud sync, data is transmitted and stored via Apple iCloud. Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
- **Apple Vision Framework**: Used for on-device AI image recognition (text recognition, subject extraction). All processing occurs locally; data never leaves your device
- **Umeng SDK**: Used for app analytics and statistics. The Umeng SDK collects device information (device model, OS version, unique device identifier, etc.), network information, and app list information to provide statistical analysis services. Privacy Policy: https://www.umeng.com/policy

We have signed data processing agreements with these parties requiring them to handle your information at a standard no less strict than this policy.

### 4. Storage and Security

- Location: Your data is primarily stored on your device. Cloud-sync portions, if enabled, are stored in Apple iCloud
- Retention: We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes stated in this policy
- Safeguards: We use encryption in transit and access controls to protect your information

### 5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access and review your personal information
- Correct inaccurate information
- Delete your personal information (by uninstalling the App or clearing App data)
- Withdraw previously granted permissions (via iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → 图记 / Posty)
- Export a copy of your data

### 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. If you are under 13, please use the App only with the consent and supervision of a parent or guardian. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.

### 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised Effective Date. We will notify you of material changes through in-app notifications or other means.

If you have any questions, comments, or complaints about this policy, please contact:
