## 2. Free Trial

- Monthly and yearly plans include a 3-day free trial for new subscribers
- Cancel during the trial at no charge
- After the trial, the selected plan will be charged automatically

## 3. Auto-Renewal

- Subscriptions auto-renew 24 hours before the current period ends
- Renewal price is the then-current App Store price
- Successful charges extend the subscription by one period

## 4. Cancellation

To cancel:
1. Open Settings → tap your name → tap Subscriptions
2. Select the FocusAlarm subscription
3. Tap Cancel Subscription

After cancellation:
- All features remain available until the current period ends
- When the period ends, you revert to free-user limits (max 3 alarms)

## 5. Refund Policy

- Refunds follow Apple's refund policy
- You may request a refund through Apple after purchase
- We cannot process refunds directly

## 6. Restore Purchases

If you change devices or reinstall the app, use Settings → Restore Purchases to recover your previous purchases.

- Pomodoro focus timer + Live Activity
- Full soundscape library access
- Statistics dashboard
- 8 alternate app icons
- All future features included

No secondary in-app purchases required.
